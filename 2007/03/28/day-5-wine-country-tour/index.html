Day 5 – Wine Country Tour

28 Mar 2007

On Wednesday we took a Napa Valley/Sonoma Valley wine tasting tour.  The tour started from Fisherman’s Wharf and we traveled east passing AT&T Park home of the San Francisco Giants. The tour proceeded to take the San Francisco-Oakland Bay Bridge to get to the east bay area to head up to Napa Valley.

We were taken to 3 wineries in the area.

  • Kirkland Ranch Winery
  • Madonna Estate Winery
  • Viansa Winery

The tour finished by going over the Golden Gate Bridge then returning us to Fisherman’s Wharf.
