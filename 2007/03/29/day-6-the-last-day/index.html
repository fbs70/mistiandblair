Day 6 – The Last Day

29 Mar 2007

Our last day we got a late start but it was nice to sleep in. We walked around the North Beach area and took a set of stairs that went to Coit Tower.  From Coit Tower you got a great view of the whole city. We walked down Lombard Street and ran into Tattoo City, which is a tattoo shop that Ed Hardy started. We also found this really cool hat shop called Goorin. This are some of the hats that Misti and I purchased.

  • Coomers
  • Fortitude
  • Harris
  • Peaceful Valley

We finished our day by having lunch at a really cool Italian restaurant.

At 5:30pm we caught an airport shuttle which took us and 4 other passengers to San Francisco International Airport.
