Day 1 – The Arrival

24 Mar 2007

We landing at San Francisco International Airport aboard Ted of United Airlines. We took the BARTsubway from the airport into the city. We got off at the Powell St. Station. We then took the F-Line Streetcar to Pier 39. We then had to walk a few block on Beach Street to get to our hotel The Fisherman’s Wharf Sheraton.
